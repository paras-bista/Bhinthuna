# Open Graph Image Required

## Action Required:
You need to create and add an Open Graph image for social media sharing.

## Image Specifications:
- **Filename**: `og-image.jpg`
- **Location**: Place in `/public/` folder
- **Dimensions**: 1200px × 630px (recommended for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Keep under 1MB for optimal loading

## What to Include:
- Restaurant logo or branding
- Hero image of signature dish
- Restaurant name: "Bhintuna House"
- Tagline: "Authentic Burmese Cuisine in Parramatta"
- Optionally: Contact info or location

## Current References:
The following pages are configured to use this image:
- Homepage: `https://bhintunahouse.com.au/og-image.jpg`
- About: `https://bhintunahouse.com.au/og-image.jpg`
- Menu: `https://bhintunahouse.com.au/og-image.jpg`
- Catering: `https://bhintunahouse.com.au/og-image.jpg`
- Contact: `https://bhintunahouse.com.au/og-image.jpg`

## How to Test:
After adding the image:
1. Use [Facebook Sharing Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/)
2. Use [Twitter Card Validator](https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator)
3. Use [LinkedIn Post Inspector](https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/)

## Alternative:
If you don't have a custom image ready, you can use a high-quality food photo from your restaurant as a temporary solution.
